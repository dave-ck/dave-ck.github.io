Research Interests

  • Graph Theory: How can we use dots and lines to understand the world around us?
  • Computational Complexity: When are answers about our questions truly, provably out of reach? What can't we do with a computer?
  • "Eventful" Graphs: Classical graphs stay put, but the real world changes. How can we approach problems which change over time?
